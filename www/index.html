About AMYS





AMYS Martial Arts is an international family oriented martial arts school. Our main goal is to teach our students confidence, discipline, respect and focus This helps them achieve their goals and to strive for excellence in all parts of their lives. We are affiliated with the Utah State Taekwondo Association, USA-Taekwondo, Pan American Taekwondo Union, Kukkiwon and the World Taekwondo Federation.
